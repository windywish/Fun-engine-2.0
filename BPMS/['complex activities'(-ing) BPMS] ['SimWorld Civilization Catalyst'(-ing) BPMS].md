- **How to generate a religion from scratch?** - The Weaver does not spawn a "Church of Light." Instead, it initiates a **Bottom-Up World Building** process. It tracks `[suffering]` metrics in a specific region. When suffering exceeds a threshold, it enables a `['prophetic_vision']` activity for a charismatic agent. This agent shares a meme ("The Pain is a Test"). The Weaver tracks the viral spread of this meme via **Stigmergic Protocol**. If the meme helps agents survive (e.g., by encouraging communal sharing), the Weaver reinforces it. Over 100 years, this "Cult of Sharing" calcifies into a formal "Church of the Divine Test" with temples and dogma. The religion is not a setting; it is the fossilized residue of successful survival heuristics.
- **How to simulate a realistic economic crash?** - Through **Multi-Scale Resonance**. The Weaver monitors the `['grain_futures']` activity of individual merchants (Micro). It notices a feedback loop: merchants are buying grain on credit, expecting prices to rise. The Weaver allows this bubble to inflate. Then, it triggers a **Disruptive Genesis** event: "A bumper crop in the neighboring province." Prices plummet. The Weaver propagates the shockwave: Merchants default -> Banks fail -> Credit freezes -> Peasants starve. The crash is not a random event; it is the mathematical consequence of the system's previous state, executed via **Non-Linear Resonance**.
- **How to implement technological innovation?** - Via **Ecological Possibility**. The Weaver observes that the activity `['mining_coal']` produces a waste product: "Coal Tar." This creates a new **Complex Activity**: `['fashion_industry']`. The economy shifts from agrarian to industrial not because a tech tree was clicked, but because the waste of one activity became the food for another.
- **How to manage the transition from peace to war?** - The Weaver uses **Vectorial Actualization**. The **System Matrix** (the map of political tensions) shows a "Potential Well" around a disputed resource. The **System Vector** (current diplomatic relations) is drifting toward it. The Weaver does not force war. It simply stops dampening the **Phenomenological Feedback**. When a border skirmish happens, instead of suppressing the news, the Weaver amplifies it. Nationalistic fervor (Field) influences individual soldiers (Particles) to be more aggressive. The **Corpuscular-Field Duality** creates a feedback loop that slides the civilization inevitably into conflict.
- **How to ensure the history makes sense?** - Through **Fact Crystallization**. Every major event is logged as an **Atomic Fact**: `Fact(ID=992, Event="Treaty of Iron", Signers=[King_A, Queen_B], Terms=[Trade_Access])`. Future activities *must* respect these facts. History is not a story being told; it is a blockchain of constraints being built.
