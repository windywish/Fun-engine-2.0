##### **Praxial Triage: Analysis:** 

The core challenge is the translation of qualitative sensory data (taste, smell, texture) into quantitative genetic data without losing the "soul" of the culinary art. A purely mathematical approach might create "edible" but "soulless" recipes, while a purely random approach would yield chaos. The system must analyze the "chemical compatibility" of ingredients to predict the viability of a hybrid "Critter." It must solve the problem of "subjective fitness"—how to objectively rate a recipe when taste is personal. The solution lies in a "Consensus Palate" algorithm that weights user feedback against known chemical flavor pairings (e.g., molecular gastronomy databases).
