The ability to build complex organisms/systems by stacking simple, atomic instructions. A "Dragon" is just a specific stack of genes for "Scales," "Fire Breath," and "Flight."

O=∑Gi

#### **6.3. Phenomenal Stratum (Layer 3 - The Subjective Experience):**

The agent experiences "Talent" or "Nature." The innate capabilities they were "born" with.
