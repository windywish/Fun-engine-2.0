#### 5.2. The Mechanism - [The Transferable Fact]

- **The Crystallization:** When Entity B incurs a massive Thermodynamic Debt to Entity A, Entity A can choose to crystallize it into a "Favor." The Engine logs this as a specific `['atomic fact'(-ing) BPMS]` (e.g., "The Lord of the North owes his life to the Wandering Healer").

- **The Verification:** The Engine verifies the `['atomic fact'(-ing) BPMS]`. If valid, the debt is reassigned. The Lord of the North now owes his life to the Smuggler.
- **Non-Fungibility:** A Favor is not a generic coin. Its value depends entirely on the `['functional potentiality'(-ing) BPMS]` of the entity who owes the debt. A Favor from a beggar is worth less than a Favor from a dragon.

#### 5.3. The Emergent Property - [The Economy of Influence]
