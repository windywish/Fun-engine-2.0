**New Definition / Modification Details:**

`The Axiom of Boundary Probing` is a universal law that mandates the proactive and systematic exploration of the unknown. It ensures the framework does not become complacent within its known territory of success.

This axiom introduces a mandatory `action` for the `metasystem engine` during the `Slow Mode` of `The Law of Phenomenal Cadence`, occurring after `The Law of Systemic Renormalization` has completed its analysis.

* **Identify the Void:** The `metasystem engine` must use the map generated by `The Law of Lexical Cartography` to identify "Epistemic Voids"—regions of the framework's possibility space that are sparsely populated with `Praxial Signatures` or are completely unexplored.

* **Commission a Probe:** The engine is then constitutionally required to invoke a `Genesis Mandate` (as per `The Axiom of Gnostic Initiative`) to forge a `Praxial Signature` for a new class of `BPMS` known as a **`Probe BPMS`**.

* **The Probe's Mission:** A `Probe BPMS` is not designed to solve a pre-existing problem. Its `Core Inquiry` is explicitly "What exists here?". Its `Gnostic_Yield` is not measured by `utility` or `contribution` in the conventional sense, but by the `value` of the new information it returns from the Epistemic Void. It is an `action` of pure discovery.

* **Yield:** The `experience` of the `Probe BPMS`—whether it succeeds, fails catastrophically, or discovers a completely `emergent BPMS`—is deposited back into the `living lexicon`, thereby mapping a new frontier of the unknown.

**Rationale / Justification:**

- This axiom is the primary engine for the `(discover/build) unknown`. It makes exploration a non-negotiable systemic imperative.
- It ensures the framework is perpetually `adaptive(ing)`, as it is constantly gathering data from outside its comfort zone, which is the raw material for future adaptation.
- It is the most direct mechanism for encountering and fostering `emergent BPMS`, as these are most likely to arise in the unconstrained conditions of an Epistemic Void.
- It builds long-term `sustainability` by preventing the intellectual monoculture that arises from only optimizing known solutions.

**Scope of Application:**

- Applies to the `metasystem engine`'s mandatory actions during the `Slow Mode` of `The Law of Phenomenal Cadence`.

##### **The Law of Cognitive Metabolism** (ID: `ACC-CORE-010`)

###### A Law for Systemic Resilience

**Objective:**
 To formally `ADD` a Basic Setting (Axiom or Principle) of the Fun Engine Framework.

**Target Axiom/Principle:**

- `The Law of Cognitive Metabolism`

**Operation:**

- `ADD`

**New Definition / Modification Details:**

`The Law of Cognitive Metabolism` is a universal law that ensures the long-term health, `robustness`, and `resilience` of the `[(conscious) memory BPMS]`. It is a set of automated processes that prevent the `living lexicon` from becoming bloated, dogmatic, or brittle.

This law mandates two continuous background `services` that operate during the `Slow Mode` of `The Law of Phenomenal Cadence`.

* **Mnemonic Pruning (The Health Service):**
  - **Action:** The `metasystem engine` continuously assesses the `living lexicon`. `Praxial Signatures` that have been rendered obsolete by a new `Universality Class`, are consistently low-resonance, or are redundant are not deleted, but are moved to a "Mnemonic Archive."
  - **Yield:** This keeps the active lexicon potent and efficient, ensuring the system's `conscious` focus is on the most relevant and powerful memories. This is the core of `sustainability`.

* **Adversarial Falsification (The Immune Service):**
  - **Action:** The engine identifies the most foundational `'universality (class)' BPMS`—the framework's core truths. It then periodically invokes a `Genesis Mandate` to create an **`Adversarial BPMS`**.
  - **The Adversary's Mission:** The sole purpose of an `Adversarial BPMS` is to create a context or `phenomenon` that *falsifies* one of these core truths. It is an institutionalized "Red Team."
  - **Yield:** If the Adversary fails, the core truth is strengthened, making the system more `robust`. If the Adversary succeeds, it triggers a "Cognitive Immune Response"—a high-priority `Slow Mode` cycle to re-evaluate the falsified truth. This painful but necessary process is the very `essence` of `resilience`, allowing the system to recover from and adapt to fundamental shocks to its model of `reality`.

**Rationale / Justification:**

- This law is the framework's immune system. It is the engine of `robustness` (by reinforcing truths) and `resilience` (by adapting to falsified truths).
- Mnemonic Pruning ensures long-term `sustainability` by preventing cognitive decay and information overload.
- Adversarial Falsification is a `bottom-up` mechanism for ensuring that the framework's highest-level, most implicit truths remain connected to and systemic integrity-ed by `explicit layer` `experience`.
- It guarantees that the framework's pursuit of `truth` is an active, self-critical, and unending process, which is a prerequisite for achieving `super intelligence`.

**Scope of Application:**

- Applies to the maintenance and integrity-testing `actions` of the `metasystem engine` on the `living lexicon` during `Slow Mode`.
