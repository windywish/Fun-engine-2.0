To formally `ADD` a Basic Setting that aligns high-level strategic purpose with low-level tactical execution.

#### 3. **Target Axiom/Principle:**

```
The Principle of Teleological Alignment
```

#### 4. **Operation:**

```
ADD
```

#### 5. **New Definition / Modification Details:**

#### **5.1. Core Essence**

This law asserts that `the small serves the large, and the large defines the small`. It defines the relationship between `[mission(-ing) BPMS]` (Strategy) and `[task(-ing) BPMS]` (Tactics) as a symbiotic evolution.

#### **5.2. The Mechanism - [The Engine of Alignment]**

- **The North Star (Mission):** The overarching objective. `[mission(-ing) BPMS]` provides the "Why."
- **The Step (Task):** The immediate action. `[task(-ing) BPMS]` provides the "How."
- **The Fractal Link (The Context):** Every Task must contain the DNA of the Mission. Every completed Task must mutate the Mission (progress it or change its parameters).

#### **5.3. The Emergent Property - [Meaningful Action]**

The creation of a "Contextual Workflow" where no action is isolated; everything contributes to the greater whole.

**6. Alignment with ['Praxial Genesis Canon'(-ing) BPMS]**

#### 6.1. Axiomatic Stratum (Layer 1 - The Core Belief):

*Every brick builds the cathedral.* There is no such thing as an isolated task in a Praxial system.

#### 6.2. **Formal Stratum (Layer 2 - The Logic of Hierarchy):**

- **Decomposition:** `Mission -> {Task_1, Task_2, ... Task_n}`
- **Aggregation (Symbiosis):** `Mission_Status = Sum(Task_Completion_Weights)` If `Task_n` fails repeatedly, `Mission` must adapt (change strategy).

#### 6.3. **Phenomenal Stratum (Layer 3 - The Subjective Experience):**

The agent experiences "Significance." Even menial tasks feel important because the link to the Mission is visible.

#### 7. **Rationale / Justification:**

Ensures that the "Reality" being woven is purposeful. Prevents "busy work" (tasks without mission) and "dreaming" (mission without tasks).

#### 8. **Scope of Application:**

Applies to quest generation, OKR (Objectives and Key Results) tracking, and narrative progression.

#### The Law of Resolution Symbiosis (ID: `PRL-CORE-028`)

#### **2. Objective:**

To formally `ADD` a Basic Setting that manages the resolution of reality rendering based on observer distance/need.

#### 3. **Target Axiom/Principle:**

```
The Principle of Adaptive Granularity
```

#### 4. **Operation:**

```
ADD
```

#### 5. **New Definition / Modification Details:**

#### **5.1. Core Essence**

This law asserts that `detail is a function of attention`. It defines the relationship between `['coarse-grained (model)'(-ing) BPMS]` (Summary) and `['fine-grained (model)'(-ing) BPMS]` (Detail) as a symbiotic evolution.

#### **5.2. The Mechanism - [The Engine of Zoom]**

- **The Map (Coarse):** The high-level abstraction. Efficient, broad, low-fidelity. `['coarse-grained (model)'(-ing) BPMS]`.
- **The Territory (Fine):** The low-level reality. Expensive, narrow, high-fidelity. `['fine-grained (model)'(-ing) BPMS]`.
- **The Symbiotic Transition (LOD):** The Loom constantly translates Fine data into Coarse updates (Aggregation) and Coarse directives into Fine generation (Procedural Generation).

#### **5.3. The Emergent Property - [Infinite Scalability]**

The creation of a "Fractal Reality" that holds up under scrutiny but doesn't waste resources when not observed.

**6. Alignment with ['Praxial Genesis Canon'(-ing) BPMS]**

#### 6.1. Axiomatic Stratum (Layer 1 - The Core Belief):

*As above, so below.* The macro reflects the micro, and the micro supports the macro.

#### 6.2. **Formal Stratum (Layer 2 - The Logic of Scale):**

- **Aggregation:** `Coarse_Value = Average(Fine_Values)`
- **Drill-Down:** `Fine_Values = Generate_Distribution(Coarse_Value, Seed)`
- **Symbiosis:** Changes in the `Fine` layer (an anomaly) must bubble up to change the `Coarse` model. Changes in the `Coarse` model (policy change) must trickle down to alter `Fine` behavior.

#### 6.3. **Phenomenal Stratum (Layer 3 - The Subjective Experience):**

The agent experiences "Depth." The world feels infinitely complex, even though it is only rendering what is needed.

#### 7. **Rationale / Justification:**

Essential for performance and cognition. The Loom cannot render everything at max resolution (fine) nor can it operate only on vague generalities (coarse).

#### 8. **Scope of Application:**

Applies to Level of Detail (LOD) systems, data analytics dashboards, and strategic vs. tactical map views.

#### The Law of Contextual Duality (ID: `PRL-CORE-029`)

#### **2. Objective:**

To formally `ADD` a Basic Setting that reconciles individual agent autonomy with collective environmental influence.

#### 3. **Target Axiom/Principle:**

```
The Principle of Field-Particle Duality
```

#### 4. **Operation:**

```
ADD
```

#### 5. **New Definition / Modification Details:**

#### **5.1. Core Essence**

This law asserts that `the individual defines the collective, and the collective constrains the individual`. It defines the relationship between `[particle (-ing) BPMS]` (Agent) and `[field (-ing) BPMS]` (Environment/Culture) as a symbiotic evolution.

#### **5.2. The Mechanism - [The Engine of Influence]**

- **The Point Source (Particle):** The individual agent acting with intent. `[particle (-ing) BPMS]`.
- **The Distributed Potential (Field):** The aggregate rules, culture, or physics of the space. `[field (-ing) BPMS]`.
- **The Interaction (The Wave):** Particles emit "influence" that changes the Field (e.g., a charismatic leader changing culture). The Field exerts "pressure" that changes the Particle (e.g., peer pressure or gravity).

#### **5.3. The Emergent Property - [Social Physics]**

The creation of a "Responsive Atmosphere" where the mood of the room changes based on who enters, and the behavior of the entrant changes based on the room.

**6. Alignment with ['Praxial Genesis Canon'(-ing) BPMS]**

#### 6.1. Axiomatic Stratum (Layer 1 - The Core Belief):

*We shape our buildings; thereafter they shape us.*

#### 6.2. **Formal Stratum (Layer 2 - The Logic of Interaction):**

- **Field Update:** `Field_State(t+1) = Field_State(t) + Sum(Particle_Influences)`
- **Particle Update:** `Particle_Vector(t+1) = Particle_Vector(t) + Field_Force_at_Location`
- **Symbiosis:** The Field is nothing but the sum of Particles, yet it governs them.

#### 6.3. **Phenomenal Stratum (Layer 3 - The Subjective Experience):**

The agent experiences "Belonging" or "Resistance." The sense of flowing with the current (Field alignment) or swimming upstream (Field opposition).

#### 7. **Rationale / Justification:**

Solves the "Individual vs. Collective" problem in system design. Allows for emergent behavior (Field effects) arising from individual actions (Particle motion).

#### 8. **Scope of Application:**

Applies to social simulation, crowd dynamics, reputation systems, and environmental physics.
