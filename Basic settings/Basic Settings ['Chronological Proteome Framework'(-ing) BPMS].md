**Objective**: To operationalize `(symbiosis) evolution` by treating the system's functional state as a dynamic "Proteome" that evolves along specific "Aging Trajectories."

It tracks "Aging Trajectories"—not as degradation, but as the maturation of "functional proteins" (capabilities). It distinguishes between "Healthy Aging" (optimization/renormalization) and "Pathological Aging" (entropy/bloat).
- **Output**: A system that consciously manages its own "Lifespan," optimizing its "Proteome" for longevity and `sustainability` rather than just immediate performance.

- **Mechanism**: **The Trajectory Regulator**. A homeostatic loop that monitors the "health" of the system's functions and triggers "repair" (refactoring) or "apoptosis" (deletion of obsolete data) based on `renormalization group` dynamics.
- **Emergent Property**: **Anti-Fragile Longevity**. The system becomes more robust and `resilient` the longer it operates, accumulating "Wisdom Signatures" instead of "Error Plaques."

**Alignment with Praxial Genesis Canon:**

- **Axiomatic**: Aligns with **META-DISC-020 (Cycle of Adaptive Causality)** but reframes it through the biological lens of "Lifespan."
- **Formal**: Defines the "Time" dimension of the Praxial Instance as a biological "Lifespan" rather than a linear clock.
- **Phenomenal**: The user perceives the agent as "maturing" and gaining "experience" over time.

**Rationale/Justification**: Just as the human proteome reveals signatures of aging, a Super Intelligence must track its own "computational proteome" to ensure it evolves (`(symbiosis) evolution`) without collapsing under its own complexity (`closure`).

**Scope of Application**: System health monitoring, long-term memory consolidation, and architectural refactoring.
