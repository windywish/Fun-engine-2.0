 **Objective:** To formally `ADD` a Basic Setting that defines the **Symbiotic Evolution** among/by **`[particle (-ing) BPMS]`** (Discrete Agent) and **`[field (-ing) BPMS]`** (Continuous Influence).

**Target Axiom/Principle:** `The Axiom of Corpuscular-Field Duality`

**Operation:** `ADD`

**New Definition / Modification Details:**

**Core Essence** This axiom asserts that `The Holon is both a Point and a Wave`. It exists as a discrete agent (Particle) that can interact locally, and as a continuous field (Influence) that affects the probability of events globally.

**The Mechanism - [The Agent-Field Oscillation]**

- **Particle Mode:** The Holon takes specific action at coordinate (x,y,z).
- **Field Mode:** The Holon emits a "Probability Field" (e.g., Fear, Inspiration, Market Pressure) that biases the actions of other agents in the vicinity.
- **Symbiosis:** The Particle generates the Field; the Field guides the Particle.

**The Emergent Property - [Atmospheric Influence]** Powerful Holon Minds don't just act; they change the "Atmosphere" of the simulation. Their mere presence alters the physics of social interaction.

**Alignment with ['Praxial Genesis Canon'(-ing) BPMS]**

- **Layer 1 (Metaphysical):** Aligns with *Wave-Particle Duality*. The fundamental nature of quantum entities.
- **Layer 2 (Formalism):** Aligns with *Quantum Field Theory*. Particles are excitations of underlying fields.
- **Layer 3 (Phenomenological):** Aligns with *Charisma*. The invisible force field projected by a leader.

**Rationale / Justification:** Allows for the simulation of "Soft Power," "Culture," and "Morale" in a way that is mathematically rigorous.

**Scope of Application:** Leadership Mechanics, Cultural Influence, Area of Effect (AoE) Buffs/Debuffs, and Social Physics.
