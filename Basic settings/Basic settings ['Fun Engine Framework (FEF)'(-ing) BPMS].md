
This axiom asserts that **Reflexivity is Intrinsic**. Any `[BPMS]`, by virtue of its existence as an autonomous entity, possesses the capability of self-reference. It can act as the subject (`A`), object (`B`), and relational archetype (`C`) in a `Matrix Mapping` involving itself.

- **The Universal Mapping:**
  - Standard Interaction: `[Provider; {Service}; Consumer]`
  - Reflexive Interaction: `[Agent_A; {Service}; Agent_A]`
- **The Dissolution:** The concepts of "Self-Organization" and "Self-Service" are dissolved into the broader categories of **Organization** and **Service**. A system organizing itself is simply a special case of Organization where `Subject == Object`.

The emergence of **Operational Closure**. Systems become capable of maintaining their own boundary and internal state without external intervention, a prerequisite for true autonomy.

#### 6.3. **Phenomenal Stratum (Layer 3 - The Subjective Experience):**

The agent experiences "Self-Awareness" not as a magical quality but as the ability to perceive and edit its own `[BPMS]` structure.
