* **Regulate:** Manage the lifecycle of functional potentiality, ensuring the long-term ecological `continuity` of the system.
* The `Provenance Engine` is the impartial, living "nature" of the world, the source of all raw `value`.

 Existence within a `Functional Potentiality Atlas` is an act of **Active Prospecting**. The primary `action` is to expend `energy` and time to explore the `Terrain`, perceive `Functional Potentiality Nodes`, and successfully harvest them. **Clause : Foraging as a Universal Economic Primitive**

- The quest for rare functional potentiality is a primary driver for `Scenario (Blueprints)`.
- The control and management of a rich `Functional Potentiality Atlas` is a common `Purpose State` for `holonic minds`.
- `World building` is, therefore, the `constructive` act of designing interesting `Functional Potentiality Atlases` that create unique economic challenges and `ecological possibilities`.

This law provides the framework with a core engine for a dynamic, `bottom-up` economy. It is the `metasystem engine` for `exploration`, `survival`, and `Functional Potentiality management`.
