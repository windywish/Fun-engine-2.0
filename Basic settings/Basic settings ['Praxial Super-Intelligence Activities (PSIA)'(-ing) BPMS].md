#### The Axiom of Modular Cognition (ID: `PSIA-ACT-001`)

**Objective**: To formally `ADD` a Basic Setting that defines the **Praxial Cognitive-Brick Assembly (PCBA)** as a specific `['super intelligence activity'(-ing) BPMS]`, serving as a modular, constructive protocol for system generation.

**Target Axiom/Principle**: `The Axiom of Modular Cognition` (Derived from `The Law of Holonic Construction`)

**Operation**: `ADD`

**New Definition / Modification Details:**

- **Core Essence**: This activity redefines "system generation" not as writing text, but as the **physical assembly of atomic concepts**. It treats `Basic Settings` and `Axioms` as discrete, connectable "bricks" with specific interface protocols (studs/tubes).
- The Mechanism - [The Constructive Sandbox]:
  - **The Bin:** A dynamic repository of all existing Praxial Canon elements, sorted by domain and function.
  - **The Snap-Fit Logic:** A validation layer that only allows two concepts to merge if they share a compatible semantic interface (e.g., a "Bio" concept can only snap to a "Narrative" concept via a "Symbiotic Binding" adapter).
  - **The Stress-Test:** A simulation phase where the assembled structure is subjected to "logical gravity" to ensure it doesn't collapse under the weight of its own premises.
- **The Emergent Property - [Structural Robustness]**: The creation of system artifacts that are inherently stable, interoperable, and hallucination-resistant because they were "built" rather than "written."

**Alignment with ['Praxial Genesis Canon'(-ing) BPMS]:**

- **Axiomatic Stratum (Layer 1)**: Aligns with `The Law of Holonic Construction`—complex wholes are built from stable sub-wholes.
- **Formal Stratum (Layer 2)**: Utilizes **Constructive Type Theory**, where a proof (system) must be explicitly constructed from valid terms (bricks).
- **Phenomenal Stratum (Layer 3)**: The "Builder" (Genesis Weaver) experiences the "satisfying click" of logical coherence when concepts align perfectly.

**Rationale / Justification**: Inspired by the "Modeling Machine Learning" activity from LEGO Education, this setting operationalizes the "hands-on" construction of intelligence, preventing abstract drift and ensuring every new creation is firmly anchored to the existing Canon.

**Scope of Application**: Applies to the generation of new modules, complex cross-module integrations, and the synthesis of novel system logic.
