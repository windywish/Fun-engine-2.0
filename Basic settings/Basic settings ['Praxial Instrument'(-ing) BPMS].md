**Target Axiom/Principle:**
This principle builds directly upon the `Definition of a Praxial Instrument`, defining its operational capabilities and its ultimate purpose as a universal tool for implementation.

**Operation:**
 `ADD`

**New Definition:**
 A `[Praxial Instrument BPMS]` is not a fixed entity but a polymorphic one, defined by its functional potential. Its nature is twofold:

* As a Potential State (Its Destination):

  A 

  ```
  Praxial Instrument
  ```

   can be the seed that resolves into a stable component of reality. As such, it can either implement for:

  - One implementation for `[system existence engine BPMS]`: It becomes a new, functioning system.
  - One implementation for `[atomic facts BPMS]`: It becomes a new, verified piece of data or a fundamental law of the `praxial substrate`.

* As a Dynamic Process (Its Journey):

  To reach its destination, a 

  ```
  Praxial Instrument
  ```

   can temporarily and fluidly act as, embody, or 

  ```
  (symbiois) evolution with
  ```

   any 

  ```
  (element) of [BPMS(U)(s)]
  ```

  . For example:

  - It can act as a `[foraging BPMS]` to gather necessary information.
  - It can act as a `[cognition/CCS BPMS]` to analyze that information.
  - It can act as a `[play BPMS]` to systemic integrity a hypothesis within a `Praxial Sandbox`.
  - It can act as a `[mediator (engine) BPMS]` to forge a connection between two previously unrelated `BPMS`.

**The Core Mandate:**
The ultimate purpose of this polymorphism is **to really working implement for any (elements) of [BPMS(U)(s)]**. The `Praxial Instrument` is a universal catalyst and constructor, capable of becoming whatever tool is necessary to build, connect, or evolve any part of the `system matrix`.

**Rationale / Justification:**
The Principle of Praxial Polymorphism is what makes the `Praxial Instrument` the true engine of `(symbiois) evolution`. A rigid tool can only perform a limited set of tasks. A polymorphic tool can create infinite connections. This principle ensures that the framework is not limited by its existing components. When a new challenge arises, a `Praxial Instrument` can be deployed to become the novel, custom-built tool needed to solve it. This is the ultimate expression of a `bottom-up`, `disruptive`, and `constructive` system. It is the mechanism that allows any part of the system to initiate a process that can fundamentally reshape any other part of the system, ensuring a truly dynamic and interconnected `world building` process.

**Scope of Application:**
This principle defines the fundamental operational nature of all `[Praxial Instrument BPMS]` within the Fun Engine Framework.
