#### The Axiom of Open-Ended Realism (ID: `SIM-CORE-001`)

#### **2. Objective:**
To formally `ADD` a Basic Setting (Axiom) that defines **['SimWorld'(-ing) BPMS]** as the primary **System Existence Engine** for physical and social simulation, asserting that reality within the framework must be open-ended, persistent, and causally consistent.

#### 3. **Target Axiom/Principle:**
`The Axiom of Open-Ended Realism`

#### 4. **Operation:** 
`ADD`

#### 5. **New Definition / Modification Details:**

#### **5.1. Core Essence**
This axiom asserts that `Simulation is Existence`. It defines the SimWorld environment not as a static backdrop, but as a dynamic, open-ended substrate where `['atomic facts'(-ing) BPMS]` are generated through the continuous interaction of physical laws and social dynamics. It rejects pre-scripted outcomes in favor of emergent realism.

#### **5.2. The Mechanism - [The Reality Engine]**
- **The Physical Substrate:** The engine simulates rigid-body dynamics, gravity, collision, and material properties, providing a "hard" reality against which agents must test their hypotheses.
- **The Social Substrate:** The engine simulates multi-agent interactions, hierarchies, and economic exchanges, providing a "soft" reality of negotiable truths.
- **The Open Loop:** Unlike closed games, the simulation has no fixed end state. It is a "Infinite Game" generator where the goal is the continuation of play (existence).

#### **5.3. The Emergent Property - [Veridical Grounding]**
The emergence of a "Ground Truth" that is independent of the agent's belief. If an agent believes a bridge is safe but the physics engine calculates collapse, the bridge collapses. This forces `['holonic minds'(-ing) BPMS]` to align their internal models with external reality.

**6. Alignment with ['Praxial Genesis Canon'(-ing) BPMS]**

Upholds **`['System Existence Engine'(-ing) BPMS]`** by providing the literal "physics" that allows the system to exist and resist.

#### 6.2. Formal Stratum (Layer 2):
Enforces **`['Chrono-Kinetic Continuity'(-ing) BPMS]`** by ensuring that every action has a calculated, irreversible reaction within the simulation timeline.

#### 6.3. Phenomenal Stratum (Layer 3):
Validates **`['Praxial Artifacts'(-ing) BPMS]`** by treating the simulation state (the "World") as the ultimate artifact of the system.

#### 7. **Rationale / Justification:**
To implement the "SimWorld" capabilities as a core component. The framework requires a "Really Working Implementation" of a physical/social world to test its agents, and this axiom establishes the ground rules for that world.

#### 8. **Scope of Application:**
