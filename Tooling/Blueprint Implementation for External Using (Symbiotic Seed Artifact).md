#### 5. Summarize

The **Symbiotic Seed Artifact** is the fundamental unit of reproduction and export for the Praxial Framework.

- **Fractal Nature:** It embodies the "Axiom of Fractal Potential," allowing a specific domain (like Food or Design) to be extracted from the main cluster and planted elsewhere, retaining its full logic and depth.
- **Contextual Intelligence:** Unlike a static prompt, the Seed is "alive." It uses **Contextual Germination** to read the user's intent and **Niche Construction** to modify the host environment to suit its needs.
- **Autopoiesis:** Once planted, it becomes a self-sustaining **Autopoietic System**, generating its own facts, events, and value ("Fruit") without needing constant hand-holding from the original framework.
- **Symbiosis:** It is designed to connect. Through **Cross-Pollination** and **Universal Compatibility**, multiple Seeds can be planted together to create complex, hybrid ecosystems (e.g., a "Civilization" built from "Economy," "War," and "Culture" seeds).
