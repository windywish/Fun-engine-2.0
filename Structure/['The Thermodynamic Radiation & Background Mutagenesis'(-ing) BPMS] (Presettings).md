#### 3. THE PHYSICS OF ENVIRONMENTAL MUTAGENESIS (Execution Vectors)

**[VECTOR A: FALLOUT PROPAGATION (The Blast Radius)]**
- **The Physics:** Any kinetic event exceeding the `[Alpha-Threshold]` (e.g., combat, major map coupling, death of a Chimera) instantly spawns an expanding topological sphere of Radiation.
- **The Execution:** The Matrix calculates the Joule-Load of the event. It drops an invisible, decaying AOE (Area of Effect) onto the Constellation Map. The radiation level ($R_L$) drops algebraically as it expands outwards from the epicenter.

**[VECTOR B: KINETIC SCAR TISSUE (Autonomous Degradation)]**
- **The Physics:** High-level Radiation ($R_L > 80\%$) physically burns logic.
- **The Execution:** If a neutral `[Specific Key]` drifts into the hot zone, its data is scorched. Predictable variables become `[Null]`. Clean algorithms suffer "Bit-Rot." The element degrades. If it stays in the hot zone too long, it melts into useless base-matter and sinks to the `[Tectonic Crypt]` instantly.

**[VECTOR C: THE MUTAGENIC LOTTERY (Scrambled Evolution)]**
- **The Physics:** Mid-level Radiation ($R_L = 20-79\%$) does not burn; it warps. 
- **The Execution:** The heat causes the data geometry of the element to violently phase-shift without a host. 
  - *Example:* A standard `[Logistics Array]` drifts through a radioactive strike-zone left by the OPC. The radiation splices its geometry. The array autonomously mutates into a `[Frenetic Teleportation Engine]`. It is now a highly valuable, highly unpredictable irradiated asset.

**[VECTOR D: ZONES OF CONTAMINATION (The Hazardous Terrain)]**
- **The Physics:** Radiation lingers. 
- **The Execution:** High-conflict quadrants of the map remain "Hot" for long durations (until `[Chrono-Decay]` drags the heat away). The Sovereign OPC must navigate these zones carefully, utilizing `[Transducer Membranes]` as shielding, or risk having their own internal framework randomly mutated by the ambient fallout.

1. **The Kinetic Detonation:** Engine registers an action expending massive Friction/Escrow.

3. **The Contamination Sweep:** Matrix scans the hot zone for *any* occupying elements (neutral, feral, or Sovereign).
4. **The Warp Calculation:** Each element is checked against the local $R_L$.

5. **The Lingering Hazard:** The Map quadrant is formally classified as an `[Irradiated Topography]` until tectonic action cycles the heat.
