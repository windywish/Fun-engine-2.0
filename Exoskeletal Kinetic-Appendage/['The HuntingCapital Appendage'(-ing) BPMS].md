#### 2. The 64-BPMS Adaptive Coupling Physics

1. **`[Engine 58: Dynamic Barter & Contract Forge]` + `[Engine 59: Exoskeletal Kinetic-Appendage]`:** The Matrix locks 500 Joules of the OPC's energy in Escrow to physically forge the new tool. An automated, tentacle-like agentic swarm is synthesized and grafted onto the OPC's economic output port.
2. **`[Engine 21: Blind Pheromone-Gradient Broadcasting]`:** The appendage physically dips into the topological dark forest, tasting the sociological and economic "air" for capital concentrations, client pain-points, or un-mined affordances.
3. **`[Engine 36: The Thermodynamic Guillotine]`:** The built-in fail-safe. If the appendage fails to capture prey (clients/capital) within 5,000 ticks, it is violently severed. The OPC loses the 500 Joules, but avoids the legacy "SaaS subscription bleed" of paying for software/employees that generate zero ROI.
