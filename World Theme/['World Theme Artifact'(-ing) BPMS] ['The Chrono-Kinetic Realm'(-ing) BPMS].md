### 1. Thematic Core

- **Genre:** **Kinetic Temporal-Punk**
- **Mood:** **Urgent, Resonant, Consequential.** The world hums with the vibration of things happening. Silence is entropy; noise is life.
- **Central Conflict:** **Stasis vs. Momentum.** The universe is slowly freezing into a "Zero-Event State" (Heat Death). The inhabitants, known as "Vectors," must continuously generate high-magnitude `events` to keep the timeline flowing and prevent the "Great Pause."
- Symbiotic Alignment:
  - *Derived from `EVT-CORE-001` (Discrete Actuality):* The theme emphasizes that reality is granular. The world "stutters" when events are scarce and "flows" when they are abundant.
  - *Derived from `EVT-CORE-017` (Constructive Utility):* Action is the only currency. Passive existence is a extinction sentence.

### 2. Physics & Metaphysics

- The Law of Impact Time:
  - *Definition:* Time does not pass linearly; it passes *impactfully*. A day with no `events` is skipped instantly. A second with a massive explosion lasts for an hour.
  - *Source:* `The Axiom of Discrete Actuality (EVT-CORE-001)` & `The Axiom of Chrono-Symbiosis (EVT-CORE-023)`.
- The Ripple Mechanics:
  - *Definition:* Every action emits visible "Resonant Constellation Map Ripples." A punch throws a shockwave that can knock over a vase three rooms away if the "Resonance" is right.
  - *Source:* `The Axiom of Resonant Constellation Map Resonance (EVT-CORE-002)`.
- Narrative Gravity:
  - *Definition:* Objects and people with high `Narrative Weight` possess physical gravity. A King or a Hero literally pulls the environment towards them. Important moments feel "heavy."
  - *Source:* `The Axiom of Narrative Weight (EVT-CORE-003)`.
- The Retcon Field:
  - *Definition:* The past is semi-fluid. Revealing a secret (`Implicit Closure`) can physically reshape the architecture of a building or the scar on a face.
  - *Source:* `The Axiom of Retroactive Continuity (EVT-CORE-004)` & `The Axiom of Implicit Closure (EVT-CORE-025)`.
- Kinetic Vitality:
  - *Definition:* To stop moving is to fade from existence. Entities that do not participate in `events` become transparent and eventually de-rez.
  - *Source:* `The Axiom of Kinetic Existence (EVT-CORE-027)`.

### 3. Geography & Locations

- The Event Horizon Citadel:
  - *Description:* A massive fortress located at the center of the "Moment." It is the only place where `Time` is stable, powered by the `Metasystem Engine`.
  - *Function:* The seat of the "Chronarchs" who regulate the `Global Event Threshold` (`EVT-CORE-013`).
- The Echo Wastes:
  - *Description:* Vast, grey plains where `events` have ceased. Here, `atomic facts` decay, and reality is blurry (`coarse-grained`).
  - *Source:* `The Axiom of Resolution Scaling (EVT-CORE-011)`.
- The Resonance Canyons:
  - *Description:* Deep valleys carved not by water, but by the "Resonant Constellation Map Ripples" of ancient wars. The echoes of those battles still physically hurt travelers.
  - *Source:* `The Axiom of Field Excitation (EVT-CORE-012)`.
- The Flashbulb Archives:
  - *Description:* A crystalline library where "Core Memories" are stored as frozen statues.
  - *Source:* `The Axiom of Mnemonic Imprint (EVT-CORE-005)`.
- The Niche Frontier:
  - *Description:* A shifting borderland of raw "Possibility Space." Here, adventurers can spawn new mountains or rivers simply by performing `Constructive Events`.
  - *Source:* `The Axiom of Ecological Actualization (EVT-CORE-018)`.
