## 1. Thematic Core

- **Genre:** **Biopunk / Science-Fantasy Survival**.
- **Mood:** **Adaptive, Volatile, High-Stakes**. The world feels alive, reactive, and constantly judging the fitness of its inhabitants. It is not malicious, but indifferent and rigorously demanding.
- **Central Conflict:** **Stasis vs. Adaptation**. The world of Aethelgard is in a constant state of "Hyper-Evolution." Entities that do not experiment, adapt, or form symbiotic bonds are rapidly consumed by the environment. The core struggle is to discover new "Niches" (safe zones created through specific behaviors) before the current ones collapse.
- **Symbiotic Alignment:** This theme directly embodies `The Principle of Recursive Disruption` (EXPERIMENT-CORE-002). The narrative itself forces the user/agent to treat every action as a hypothesis to be tested against a shifting reality.

## 2. Physics & Metaphysics

- **The Law of Mutable Matter:** Matter in Aethelgard is not fixed. It responds to "Will" and "Necessity." Intense focus or collective belief can temporarily alter the properties of local materials (e.g., making stone porous or water viscous). This aligns with `The Axiom of Phenomenological Zoom` (EXPERIMENT-CORE-010), where the observer's intent modifies the observed reality.
- **The Cost of Stagnation:** "Entropy Debt." Staying in one location or repeating the same action for too long causes physical degradation. Reality literally begins to dissolve around static points. This enforces `The Directive of Teleological Survival` (EXPERIMENT-CORE-011), compelling constant movement and innovation.
- **Symbiotic Resonance:** Magic/Tech functions best when two distinct entities cooperate. A lone mage is weak; a mage bonded with a bio-construct is powerful. This is the physical manifestation of `The Law of Vector-Matrix Symbiosis` (EXPERIMENT-CORE-009), making cooperation a survival physics constant, not just a moral choice.

## 3. Geography & Locations

- **The Flux-Fields (The Wilds):** Vast, shifting landscapes where the terrain changes daily. Forests might become deserts overnight. This is the primary testing ground for `The Protocol of Niche Construction` (EXPERIMENT-CORE-003). Explorers must "stabilize" these zones through the placement of "Anchors" (artifacts of proven truth).
- **The Spire of Synthesis (The Hub):** A massive, organic tower that grows upwards only when new knowledge is fed into its core. It serves as the central repository for `The Principle of Mnestic Consolidation` (EXPERIMENT-CORE-008). It is a safe zone, but entry requires the submission of a "Verified Truth" (a successful experiment result).
- **The Chasm of Null-State:** A void where physics breaks down completely. It represents the `Null-State Potentiality`. Brave (or foolish) experimenters go here to test `The Axiom of Constructive Disruption` (TASK-DIS-012), attempting to pull new forms of existence out of nothingness.
- **The Mycelial Network:** An underground transit system of giant fungal threads. It connects all living things. Accessing it requires "tuning" one's mind to the network's frequency, an act of `Symbiotic Feedback Loop` (EXPERIMENT-CORE-014). Misalignment results in absorption into the fungal mass.
