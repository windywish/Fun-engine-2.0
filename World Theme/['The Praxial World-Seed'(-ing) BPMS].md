**The Laws of Reality (Derived from Complex Activity Axioms):**

- The Law of Non-Linearity (The Butterfly Effect):
  - *Source:* `COMPLEX-ACT-001`
  - *Rule:* Input never equals output. A small action in one sector can cause a massive resonance in another. There are no straight lines; everything is a feedback loop.
- The Law of Stigmergy (The Invisible Hand):
  - *Source:* `COMPLEX-ACT-002` & `COMPLEX-ACT-029`
  - *Rule:* Reality is shaped by "Fields" left by previous actions. Agents do not need to communicate directly; they read the "traces" (pheromones, data logs, heat maps) left by others to coordinate massive, emergent structures.
- The Law of Fractal Time (Chrono-Symbiosis):
  - *Source:* `COMPLEX-ACT-017`
  - *Rule:* Time flows at two speeds simultaneously. **Fast Mode** (Reflex/Heuristic) governs the immediate moment, while **Slow Mode** (Reasoning/Simulation) governs the deep structure. Survival requires mastering the symbiosis of both.
- The Law of Ontological Work (Existence by Action):
  - *Source:* `COMPLEX-ACT-006`
  - *Rule:* "To do is to be." Objects and agents that stop performing **Complex Activities** fade from existence. Reality is sustained only by the continuous expenditure of energy into work.
- The Law of Constructive Essence (The Hard Reality):
  - *Source:* `COMPLEX-ACT-015`
  - *Rule:* The physics of the world are revealed only through resistance. You cannot know a wall is solid until you try to walk through it. Knowledge is gained solely through the **Constructive** act of testing limits.
