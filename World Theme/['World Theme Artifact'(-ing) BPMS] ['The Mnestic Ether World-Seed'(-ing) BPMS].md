### 1. Thematic Core

- **Genre:** Epistemic Fantasy / Metaphysical Sci-Fi.
- **Mood:** Ethereal, shifting, and resonant. The atmosphere is defined by the "Weight" of history versus the "Heat" of the present. It feels like walking through a living library where the books rewrite themselves based on who is reading them.
- **Central Conflict:** **The War of Veridicality.** The struggle is between **The Crystallization** (The effort of the Living Chronicle to preserve truth and meaning) and **The Fade** (The entropy of unrecorded data/forgotten history) or **The Distortion** (Propaganda and false narratives attempting to overwrite the Atomic Fact Ledger).
- **Narrative Hook:** "In this world, if it is not written, it did not happen. And if it is written poorly, it happens wrong."

### 2. Physics & Metaphysics

- **The Axiom of Ontological Record (Physics of Existence):** Matter is condensed Information. An object's physical stability is directly proportional to the depth of its entry in the **Living Chronicle**. Unrecorded objects flicker and vanish (The Fade).
- **The Law of Narrative Causality (Metaphysics of Event):** Events do not occur merely due to kinetic cause-and-effect but due to **Narrative Necessity**. The "Service" (The Living Chronicle) acts as a god-force that bends probability to ensure coherent storytelling (e.g., "The Hero will find the sword because the Chronicle requires a climax").
- The Principle of Mnemonic Gravity:
  - **"Heavy" Zones:** Areas with deep, ancient history (Cold Tier Data) are physically dense, hard to change, and time moves slowly there.
  - **"Hot" Zones:** Areas of new activity (Hot Tier/RAM) are volatile, shifting, and malleable. Reality here is fluid until "Crystallized" by the Service.
- **The Mechanic of Veridical Feedback:** If a physical action contradicts the **Atomic Fact Ledger** (e.g., a user tries to walk through a wall that History says is solid), the reality "glitches" or rejects the action violently (The Protocol of Veridical Data Alignment).

### 3. Geography & Locations

- **The Spire of Crystallization (The Core):** The physical manifestation of the **Living Chronicle Service**. A towering structure of shifting light where the "Weaver Nodes" reside. It is the center of the world, beaming "Meaning" out to stabilize the lands.
- **The Sea of Raw Logs (The Periphery):** The chaotic borderlands where new data streams in. It is a roiling ocean of unformed potential (white noise, static). "Ingest Nodes" patrol the shores, netting "Facts" to build land.
- **The Archives of Deep Time (The Underworld):** Vast, subterranean caverns corresponding to the **Cold Tier Storage**. Here, ancient kings and dead empires lie in perfect, immutable stasis. It is dangerous to enter, as the "weight" of history can crush a visitor's mind.
- **The Glitch-Lands (The Anomalies):** Regions where **Veridical Conflicts** occurred (where Data contradicted Narrative). These are fractured zones of impossible geometry, quarantined by "Gardener Nodes" until the narrative can be repaired.
